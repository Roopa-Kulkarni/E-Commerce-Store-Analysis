# E-Commerce-Store-Analysis

Objective

To analyze Olist's e-commerce sales and operational data to identify sales trends, customer purchasing behaviour, product performance, payment patterns, delivery efficiency, and factors influencing customer satisfaction, and provide actionable insights to improve business performance.

Tools used  Excel| Tableau| Power BI | data analysis | data visualization

Key KPI'S

Weekday Vs Weekend (order_purchase_timestamp) Payment Statistics

Number of Orders with review score 5 and payment type as credit card.

Average number of days taken for order_delivered_customer_date for pet_shop

Average price and payment values from customers of sao paulo city

Relationship between shipping days (order_delivered_customer_date - order_purchase_timestamp) Vs review scores.

Key Insights

Sales showed an overall growth trend, with certain months recording significantly higher order volumes.

São Paulo emerged as one of the strongest customer markets, contributing significantly to overall sales.

Credit cards were the most preferred payment method, accounting for a major share of transactions.

Weekday purchases contributed higher overall payment activity compared with weekends.

Most customers provided positive reviews, with 5-star ratings representing a significant share of feedback.

The average delivery time was approximately 11 days, highlighting the importance of efficient logistics.

Orders with longer delivery times and delays tended to be associated with lower customer review scores.

High-demand categories generated strong order volumes, while some categories achieved higher revenue through greater average product prices

Improving delivery efficiency, optimizing freight costs, and strengthening high-performing categories can enhance customer satisfaction and overall profitability.
